About

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    United in Nursing, Unity Healthcare Solutions Inc. is a Nursing staffing agency, founded in 2018 by Monique Champoux and Carolyn Harnum. At this time, Unity is housed in East Selkirk Manitoba. Nursing assignments are situated through out Manitoba.

  • Company Vision

    To develop a quality staffing strategy which will ultimately reduce healthcare provider staffing gaps that exist within Manitoba Federal, Provincial, and Private Healthcare Systems.

  • Company Mission

    To work towards becoming a leading staffing agency for Federal, Provincial and Private Healthcare Sectors within Manitoba, by recruiting and retaining quality healthcare professionals.

Our amazing team

Monique Champoux

Co-Founder

Monique Started her nursing career in Leaf Rapids her home town, and the neighboring northern town of Lynn Lake, Mb. Monique has gained over 20 years experience working in the Northern Regional Health Authority, federal nursing stations with Health Canada, Tribal Council Healthcare, independent and band run First Nation health facilities.

Carolyn Harnum

Co-Founder

Starting her career, also in her home town of Leaf Rapids, Mb. Carolyn Harnum R.N has over 21 years of nursing experience. Carolyn has worked the majority of her experience in emergency room and intensive care units both in rural and tertiary care settings.

Monique and Carolyn decided to go into an equal partnership together, they believe that their combined experience, motivation and desire for a higher level of fulfillment in their nursing journey will lead them down paths they have not yet walked.
