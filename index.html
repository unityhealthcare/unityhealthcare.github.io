  • Our Humble Beginnings

    United in Nursing, Unity Healthcare Solutions Inc. is a Nursing staffing agency, founded in 2018 by Monique Champoux R.N and Carolyn Harnum R.N. Both Monique and Carolyn had visions of starting a Nursing agency for many years and started the planning stages approximately one year prior to picking up contracts in November 2018. The development process was a long journey but the foundation had to be strong form the beginning!

  • Company Vision

    To develop a quality staffing strategy which will ultimately reduce healthcare provider staffing gaps that exist within Manitoba Federal, Provincial, and Private Healthcare Systems.

  • Company Mission

    To work towards becoming a leading staffing agency for Federal, Provincial and Private Healthcare Sectors within Manitoba, by recruiting and retaining quality healthcare professionals.

Monique Champoux

R.N / Co-Founder

Monique Champoux started her nursing career in her home town of Leaf Rapids Mb, and the neighbouring community of Lynn Lake. Monique has over 20 years of experience working in several Manitoba federal nursing stations and health centres, the Northern Regional Health Authority, private health sector and Tribal Council healthcare. Most of Monique’s experience is in the areas of Community Health Nursing. As a First Nations individual, Monique is passionate about working in northern rural and remote communities. She has met many different healthcare professionals, all who have helped her gain knowledge and shaped her life and career in many ways. “The people and experiences encountered are beyond words”.

Carolyn Harnum

R.N / Co-Founder

Carolyn Harnum has been nursing for 21 years. Beginning of her career was spent in her home town of Leaf Rapids and then Thompson, Manitoba. Working in The Emergency Room and Intensive Care Unit, Medical, Surgical, and Paediatric wards. Careers

Unity Healthcare does not employ Nurses or Healthcare Aides, instead we work with Independent Contractors (IC’s).

We welcome resumes/CVs from all Nurses and Healthcare Aides.

Criteria to apply as an IC;

  • Complete resume with 3 references, one has to be from your most recent supervisor
  • Nurses must be in good standing with their licensing body, CRNM, CLPNM, CRPNM
  • HCA’s send in certificates
  • All IC’s are to be up date with their immunizations. Required Immunizations are as follows;
    - MMRV - Measles, Mumps, Rubella and Varicella (Chickenpox)
    - Hepatitis B series
    - Tubercullin Mantoux screen
    Tdap – Tetnus, diptheria and pertussis (whooping cough)
  • Clear criminal record check, adult and child abuse registry check and vulnerable sector search check.
  • Be up to date with BLS and CPR
    - For Nurses seeking RHA work in acute facilities, TNCC, CTAS, ACLS and PALS is an asset
    - For Nurses seeking to work in Birthing Units or OBS, NRP is a requirement
    - For ICs looking for work in LTC / PCH experience with lifting equipment is beneficial as well a safe Client Handling course.
